🪳 I-COCKROACH

"Instantly. Affordably. Locally."
India’s First Hyperlocal Digital Execution Marketplace

🚀 Overview
I-COCKROACH is a platform that connects SME businesses with verified student talent for fast, affordable digital execution. It enables businesses to outsource small tasks efficiently while providing students with real-world experience, income opportunities, and portfolio development.

❗ Problem Statement
SMEs (Businesses)
High cost of digital agencies (₹15k–₹1L/month)
Lack of access to affordable, fast execution
Difficulty finding trusted local freelancers
Students
Limited access to real-world projects
No structured earning opportunities
Lack of credibility and portfolio-building platforms

💡 Solution
A hyperlocal marketplace where:
Businesses post tasks with budget and deadlines
Students pitch and execute tasks quickly
Platform ensures trust, tracking, and payment security

Key Value Propositions
💰 Up to 80% cost savings vs agencies
⚡ Same-day task execution
🔐 Trust-based student ranking system
📍 Hyperlocal matching
⚙️ How It Works
Post Task – Business submits requirements
Get Pitches – Students respond within hours
Select Candidate – Business chooses best fit
Execute Task – Work is completed and tracked
Approve & Pay – Payment released after approval
